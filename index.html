Mission

Constructive Feedback:

Using feedback to continuously improve documentation processes and practices.

Recognition & Acknowledgement:

Valuing and acknowledging the contributions, strengths, and weaknesses of team members involved in documentation.

Innovation:

Seeking new solutions and more effective approaches to documentation without being asked.

Commitment:

Maintaining self-discipline and taking the extra mile to ensure accurate and timely documentation.

Continuous Learning:

Continuously seeking new and better ways to document care by learning from each other.

Team Support:

Stepping up for the team when faced with challenges to ensure quality documentation.

Services

Compliance Review:

Services

Ensure all documentation complies with federal, state, and local regulations, as well as industry standards and best practices.

Regularly audit documents to identify and correct any non-compliance issues.

Accuracy Verification:

Services

Verify the accuracy and completeness of patient records, treatment plans, and progress notes.

Cross-check data across multiple sources to ensure consistency and reliability.

Timeliness Monitoring:

Services

Ensure that all required documentation is completed and submitted in a timely manner.

Implement and monitor timelines for documentation to prevent delays in patient care and billing.
