Mission

Constructive Feedback:

Using feedback to continuously improve documentation processes and practices.

Recognition & Acknowledgement:

Valuing and acknowledging the contributions, strengths, and weaknesses of team members involved in documentation.

Innovation:

Seeking new solutions and more effective approaches to documentation without being asked.

Commitment:

Maintaining self-discipline and taking the extra mile to ensure accurate and timely documentation.

Continuous Learning:

Continuously seeking new and better ways to document care by learning from each other.

Team Support:

Stepping up for the team when faced with challenges to ensure quality documentation.
